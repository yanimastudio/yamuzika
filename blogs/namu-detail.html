Namusura's biography

  • 29 August 2016
  • posted by admin
  • comments (32)

Before musical career

Namusura is a Mongolian. Namusura, who has been particularly fascinated by cycling since childhood, and that had him to register as early as the first Tour of the Lake. However, because he was too young, his parents did not agree. He had missed the Lake Race. In April 2009, when he was preparing for the high school entrance examination, he immediately ran home and discussed with his parents about signing up for the 3rd Tour of the Lakes. In order to relieve the pressure of studying, his parents agreed to him. And they bought a car to guide him in training.

He had taken part in 2 cycling contests in 2009 and 2010, when he was 16 years old. Although he had got injuries at both, but the Rocky's spirit in his blood got him to reach the goal. He has been being stubborn since childhood.

"My biggest goal is to participate in the Tour de France and to be in line with my idol Armstrong. Mom, I love you!"

But he would never have a chance to do it anymore. The injuring in his leg had been too serious that got the Tour de France became a far away dream. The Rocky's one down.

Music career

2 years later, he went abroad to Volgograd, Russia, to study in Volgograd Technical State University. In here he began his second talents, singing and songwriting. He had performed on the stage of the university and had been received many compliments for his fascinating voice. He also received many invitations to perform here and there.

In probably 2015, a Vietnamese friend bring him to the room of the man who made Yaongaku (he'll be called "Yaongaku" from here, for the sake of this website), who was a novice sound engineer at the moment. They got to know each other real quick, because both like music, and Namusura's way of writing songs caught Yaongaku attention. Yaongaku asked Namusura to be his backing music composer, and Namusura agreed immediately.

The two had made a few songs together and built a good partnership along. Both are easy, so there had never been a fight between them. All that they care about is to make good songs and music. From 2015 to June 2016, Yaongaku helped Namusura to compose and record like about 15 songs.

During the recording for the second song, "Father", written by Namusura, Namusura brought to Yaongaku's room a Russian friend from the University, the name is Denis Kutepov. He was a pianist.
